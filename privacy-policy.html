Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Liveasy Logistics ("Liveasy", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal data, as well as the choices available to you regarding such data.

What Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using our services. This includes:

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number
  • Account login credentials
  • Demographic data such as company, job title, location
  • Other data you choose to provide through surveys or correspondence

We also automatically collect certain usage information when you use our website or interactive features. This includes:

  • Device data such as IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage data such as pages visited, links clicked, videos watched
  • Tracking data such as cookies and web beacons to understand site usage

How We Use Information

  • Provide and optimize our service
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Send service announcements and administrative messages
  • Notify you about updates to our offerings
  • Deliver marketing communications per your preferences
  • Comply with legal obligations and assertions of legal rights

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Legal Basis for Processing

We only process your personal data if we have a lawful basis for doing so, including:

  • To fulfill a contract with you
  • When we have your consent to do so
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To serve our legitimate interests

Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as needed to provide our services or as required by law. We may retain certain usage data for analytics purposes, but this will be aggregated and anonymized prior to storage.

Your Privacy Choices

You have options regarding the personal data we collect and how it is used:

  • You can choose not to provide certain data, but this may impact your ability to use some features
  • You can opt out of receiving marketing communications
  • You can opt out of receiving non-essential communications
  • You can review, correct, or delete your data by contacting us

Data Security

We employ data security measures to help protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction. Measures include encryption, access controls, and physical and network security. While we follow generally accepted standards to protect data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Changes to this Policy

We may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time for operational, legal or regulatory reasons. The revised Policy will apply as of the published effective date.
