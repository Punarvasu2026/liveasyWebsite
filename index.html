How is Liveasy useful?

LIVEASY is a startup that is increasing rapidly. Their aim is to organize an unorganized Logistic Industry. So that user/transporter can fulfill their needs under one roof which will also help drivers to generate more income as well as work as per their time. Through its user-friendly interface, LIVEASY has made the logistic problems so simple that transporter can fulfill their need in just one click. With and 24X7 live tracking feature users can track the location of their goods from anywhere and at any time.
