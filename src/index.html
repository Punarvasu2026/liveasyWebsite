About Us

Liveasy is TMS provider that delivers innovative freight and logistics management platforms that automate, track, and optimize end-to-end supply chain operations for businesses. Our comprehensive solutions tackle common business pain points like freight sourcing, indent management, complex finances, paper invoicing, and outdated tracking. Liveasy dashboard integrate planning, execution, settlement, and analysis to boost efficiency, visibility, and cost savings across transportation sourcing.

Automated Solutions for Your Logistics Operations

All in One Dashboard

Gain full control of your supply chain with Liveasy's smart dashboard. Access real-time data and automation tools for streamlined logistics management.

Analytics

Access vital logistics insights with our analytics feature. Monitor placement efficiency, ratings, loading/unloading times, halt durations, transit details, and turnaround for optimized operations.

Live Tracking

Enable transparent vehicle operations with SIM-based tracking. Monitor real-time vehicle locations, routes, stops, and status using integrated SIM cards and GPS technology.

Freight Sourcing and Reverse Auction

Access our platform to connect with top LSP and new opportunities. Utilize reverse auctions for efficient freight procurement and cost savings.

Indent Automation

Automate procurement workflows with our platform. Streamline indent management, approval routing, and PO generation for enhanced efficiency and control.

Electronic Proof of Delovery

Verify goods delivery with proof of delivery (POD). Access delivery receipts, signatures, and timestamps for accurate transactions.

In Plant Movement and Optimization

Enhance Plant Efficiency with Real-Time Vehicle Monitoring. Optimize In-Plant Movement for Streamlined Operations and Cost Savings.

Reconciliation of Freight

Automate freight invoice verification and rate matching. Simplify handling invoices from Logistics Service Providers (LSPs) with accurate bill verification.

Intelligent TMS Platform

Manage all your shipments efficiently across rail, road, air, and sea with our Intelligent Transportation Management System (TMS) platform. Our centralized dashboard provides real-time visibility into shipment status, routes, and performance metrics, streamlining logistics operations.
